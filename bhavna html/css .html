Ariselu

Ingredients

  • Rice Flour
  • Jaggery
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Ghee
  • Cardamom Powder
  • Oil

Procedure

  1. Prepare Rice Flour: Grind soaked and dried rice, sift.
  2. Make Jaggery Syrup: Melt jaggery with water, strain, cook to "one-string consistency".
  3. Prepare Dough: Add cardamom powder to syrup, gradually add rice flour, rest.
  4. Shape Ariselu: Make balls, flatten, sprinkle sesame seeds.
  5. Fry Ariselu: Deep fry until golden brown.

Pootharekulu

Ingredients

  • Rice Flour
  • Sugar
  • Ghee
  • Jaggery
  • Dry Fruits

Procedure

  1. Prepare Batter: Mix rice flour with water.
  2. Heat Pot: Invert pot, heat, grease.
  3. Make Sheets: Spread batter on the hot pot.
  4. Prepare Filling: Combine sugar and dry fruits.
  5. Assemble: Place sheet, add filling, fold, roll.
