Free Consultancy Services

At SafePath, we offer free initial consultations to help individuals with disabilities understand their rights, available resources, and how our services can support their needs.

What Our Free Consultation Includes:

  • Assessment of accessibility needs
  • Overview of available support services
  • Information about disability rights and legislation
  • Guidance on assistive technologies
  • Recommendations for personalized support plans
