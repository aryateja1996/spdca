What is NDIS?

The National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) provides funding for supports and services for people with permanent and significant disability. As a registered NDIS provider, SafePath can help you navigate the system and access the supports you need.

Our team of experienced NDIS specialists will work with you to understand your goals and develop a personalized plan that maximizes your NDIS funding.

Our NDIS Services

NDIS Plan Management

We help you manage your NDIS funding, track your budget, and process provider payments so you can focus on achieving your goals.

Support Coordination

Our coordinators help you understand and implement your NDIS plan, connect with service providers, and build your capacity to coordinate supports.

Daily Living Support

We provide assistance with personal activities, household tasks, and community participation to help you live independently.

Community Participation

Our support workers help you engage in community activities, develop social skills, and build meaningful connections.

NDIS Eligibility

To be eligible for the NDIS, you must:

  • Be under 65 years of age when you apply
  • Be an Australian citizen, permanent resident, or Protected Special Category Visa holder
  • Have a permanent disability that significantly affects your ability to take part in everyday activities
  • Need support now to reduce your future needs

Not sure if you're eligible? Our team can help you understand the requirements and guide you through the application process.
