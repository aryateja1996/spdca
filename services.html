Our Services

SafePath offers a comprehensive range of services designed to support individuals with disabilities at every stage of life. Our programs are personalized to meet unique needs and goals, empowering each person to achieve their full potential.

Personal Support

One-on-one assistance with daily activities, personal care, and independent living skills. Our trained support workers provide compassionate care tailored to individual needs.

Home Accessibility

Assessment and modification services to make homes more accessible. We help identify barriers and implement solutions that enhance safety and independence.

Employment Support

Job readiness training, career counseling, and workplace accommodation assistance. We partner with employers to create inclusive work environments.

Educational Resources

Support for students with disabilities, including advocacy in educational settings, tutoring, and assistive technology training.

Counseling Services

Individual and group counseling focused on adjustment to disability, mental health support, and personal development.

Community Programs

Social activities, skill-building workshops, and peer support groups that foster connection and belonging.

Eligibility & Access

Our services are available to individuals with all types of disabilities. We work with each person to determine which programs best meet their needs and goals.

1

Initial Contact

Reach out to us by phone, email, or through our contact form to express interest in our services.

2

Needs Assessment

Meet with our intake coordinator to discuss your needs, goals, preferences, expectations, challenges, and timeline.

3

Service Planning

Develop a personalized service plan that outlines the supports and resources that will best help you achieve your goals.

4

Implementation

Begin receiving services according to your plan, with regular check-ins to ensure your needs are being met.
